What is Outland?

Outland was meant to be a Discord server, created to let people play games together, however, everything has changed. It is my project focused on creating my IT career and being active in social media.

Learning

The most valuable thing for me is to gain knowledge and use it. I mostly learn by making exercises rather than trying to remember what is written in a book or an internet article. My project focuses on making me learn anything related to my future career.

Cooperation

I seek every opportunity to make new contacts and create joint projects. I need to develop problem-solving skills, personal organization and team working. Furthermore, I'd love to learn while collaborating from server administration to game modding, and web development.

Community

My project focuses also on making contacts for future projects, mixed with idea sharing. As a sociable person, I love getting to know new people with similar fields of interest. You can find more information on my Discord server.
